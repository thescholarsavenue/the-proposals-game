What is this?

We looked at all the proposals candidates put forth over the years and categorized them into various buckets. Under Social & cultural the proposals are broadly under the themes - Spring Fest, GC/Competitions, Soc-cult culture on campus and Miscellaneous (portals, information dissemination systems, etc.). The Technology and Sports & Games brackets differ from the Social & cultural bracket with Kshitij and Inter-IIT respectively replacing Spring Fest.

For SOPs and proposals of candidates for the 2016 elections, check out http://thescholarsavenue.github.io/electionhub2016

Disclaimer : This is a work in progress. If you find any discrepancy in the data, do comment below. We'll change it as soon as possible.
